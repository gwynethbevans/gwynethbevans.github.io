Website User Flow Optimization Analysis

Role: UX Research & Strategy Proposal | Client/Product: NeuroFlow

Identified and proposed solutions to a critical website usability issue where end-users were incorrectly funneled through a demo request flow intended for business prospects. Conducted competitive analysis and developed four strategic approaches to improve conversion paths.

Key Elements:

  • Analyzed 6 competitor websites (Rose Health, Amwell, Talkspace, etc.) to identify best practices
  • Researched B2B2C SaaS navigation patterns through industry resources
  • Developed detailed proposal with 4 solution approaches and implementation examples
  • Presented findings to marketing leadership with recommendation roadmap

Solution Approaches:

  • Button Approach: Prominent navigation button for end-users
  • Multi-User Resource: Segmented hub on demo page
  • Message + Link: Contextual redirect messaging
  • Intention Check: User validation pop-up

Impact:

  • Provided data-driven recommendations to reduce misdirected form submissions
  • Improved user experience by creating clearer conversion paths
  • Increased marketing efficiency by better qualifying inbound leads

Outcome:

Helped raise awareness of maternal mental health crisis while demonstrating NeuroFlow's thought leadership and innovative approach. The episode and accompanying materials provided accessible education about this critical healthcare gap.
